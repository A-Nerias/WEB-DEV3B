FAQ's

How do I create an account?
Visit the official website or community platform associated with "The Lazy Genius Way" and look for a "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button. Provide the required information, confirm your email address through the verification link sent to your email, and then log in using your new credentials.

How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, visit the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email, check for a reset link, and follow the instructions to create a new password.
